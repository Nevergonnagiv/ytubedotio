YouTube Installer - Tutorial



  1. Find your YouTube video you 
  want to install, it can be any
  type of video (comedy, action...)


  2. On that video,
  click the "Share" button and
  then press "Copy Link" 


  3. Go to the YTube.io app
  and paste the link in 
  "YouTube URL"


  4. Click submit and wait for
  the API (new form) to process.


  5. Choose your format, and then 
  click the download button.
  (ignore the pop-ups)
